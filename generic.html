What is SAE BAJA?

SAE BAJA challenges engineering students to design and build an off-terrain vehicle that will survive the severe punishment of rough terrain. The below image illustrates our journey in building this all-terrain vehicle from scratch for the first time in college history.

Building a vehicle wasn't an easy task as it involved every aspect of an organisation from Innovation to commercialization. The above illustration shows the timeline from Design Phase to the Assembly and Testing. We started off with the design phase in May 2018 that lasted for 3 months. We implemented DFM principles in our designs to attain efficinet manufacturability in terms of cost. We moved on to supplier selection and procurement of components in the mid-summer. Manufacturing included 2 phases - Outsource Manufactuing and In-house manufacturing. Majority of the power train components were outsourced for manufacturing while vehicle assembly was done in-house in the workshop. Testing phase started in December 2018 where we conducted various tests on the vehicle on harsh road conditions. We went on to participate in the competition for the first time in the college history and secured 39th position overall out of 120 participants.
